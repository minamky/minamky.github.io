Nomi

TypeScript, React Native, Supabase

Unlike typical social or networking apps, Nomi helps young adults break out of repetitive social circles by forming friendships rooted in shared curiosity rather than convenience. By matching users to teach and learn from one another, the app turns mutual skill-building into vibrant connections.

Bubble Sort

Repeatedly steps through the list, compares adjacent elements, and swaps them if they are in the wrong order

Insertion Sort

Builds final sorted array one item at a time by inserting each new element into its correct position within the sorted portion

Quick Sort

Recursively partitions the array into smaller sub-arrays around a pivot and then sorts the sub-arrays
