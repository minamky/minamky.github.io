Nomi

TypeScript, React Native, Supabase

Unlike typical social or networking apps, Nomi helps young adults break out of repetitive social circles by forming friendships rooted in shared curiosity rather than convenience. By matching users to teach and learn from one another, the app turns mutual skill-building into vibrant connections.

Onboarding

01 Set Up Profile

Select your skill preferences to personalize your experience.

03 Generate Your Bingo Board

Create your personalized bingo board and start engaging right away.

02 Guided Walkthrough

Follow a simple step-by-step introduction to learn how Nomi works.

Update Your Learning Goals

01 Search and Discover

Browse the database to find friends based on their skills and profiles, making it easy to connect with compatible friends.

02 Chat and Connect

Start conversations to establish meaningful friendships, with all your new connections organized in the friends tab.

Plan Meetups

01 Schedule and Invite

Enter your meeting's location, time, date, and length to send an invite directly in the chat, which the recipient can accept or decline with ease.

02 Manage Meetings

Edit any meeting or invite within the chat or view your upcoming meetings in the friends tab for easy organization.

Update Your Learning Goals

01 Edit Learning Milestones

Modify any learning milestones after the initial bingo generation to keep your goals aligned with your evolving interests.

02 Complete Customization

Your learning goals showcase your unique preferences, giving you full control over your educational journey.

Reward Garden

01 Track Your Mentees' Learning Goals

Mark your mentees' learning milestones as complete. Once they complete all three milestones, you've completed a teaching goal and can add it to your reward garden.

02 Showcase Your Progress

Mentees are also prompted to add their completed learning goals to their garden, gamifying the entire skill-building process.
